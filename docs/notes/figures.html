Figures gallery

Figures produced across the aimez research program, gathered here so the camera pipeline, the routing work and the validation results can be read in one place.

Camera vision pipeline

The pipeline reads public traffic-camera frames and turns them into a continuous crowd pressure field that routing can use. The stages run from camera frame through computer-vision detection to an interpolated field and a Pareto route.

Mobility and routing

These panels compare the fastest route against routes that respect measured burden, and they show how load redistributes when the network is under stress.
