O0 SMS Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-21

O0 SMS is a private operational alert channel. It sends short hard-stop notices to a single enrolled mobile number when an automated workflow needs a human decision.

Mobile numbers — no sharing / no sale

We do not share, sell, rent, or transfer mobile phone numbers to third parties or affiliates for their marketing or promotional use. Mobile numbers are used only to deliver O0 SMS operational alerts and to process replies through Twilio and US carriers as required for message delivery.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies with operational need. Recipients typically receive a few messages per day or fewer when hard-stops occur.

Message and data rates

Message and data rates may apply. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Data we store

We store the enrolled phone number and Twilio message metadata needed to deliver and verify replies. We do not send marketing SMS.

Opt out / help

Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for help.
