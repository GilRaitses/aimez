O0 SMS Terms

Last updated: 2026-07-21

O0 SMS is a private operator alert service. By enrolling your mobile number you consent to receive automated operational SMS about workflow hard-stops and to reply with short keywords (for example proceed, shrug, yes, STOP, HELP).

Message frequency varies with operational need (typically a few messages per day or fewer). Message and data rates may apply.

Reply STOP to end messages. Reply START to re-subscribe. Reply HELP for help.
