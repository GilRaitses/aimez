aimez O0 SMS — Terms and Conditions

Last updated: 2026-07-22

Program name

aimez O0 SMS (also called O0 SMS). Operated by aimez.

Product / program description

aimez O0 SMS is a private operational alert program. It sends recurring automated SMS hard-stop notices when an aimez research workflow needs a human decision. Typical message content includes a lane or wave name, a one-line summary, a recommended next step, and optional links to aimez handoff artifacts. Recipients may reply with short keywords such as proceed, shrug, or yes. This program is not marketing, not affiliate marketing, and not third-party lead generation.

Who consent is given to

Consent is given directly to aimez for the aimez O0 SMS program only. Consent is collected on the campaign-specific opt-in page https://aimez.ai/o0-sms-opt-in.html by entering a mobile number and checking an unchecked-by-default SMS consent box. Consent is not bundled into a general website registration, purchase, or unrelated agreement.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies with operational need. Recipients typically receive a few messages per day or fewer when hard-stops occur.

Message and data rates

Message and data rates may apply.

Opt out

Reply STOP to any aimez O0 SMS message to end messages. After STOP, no further aimez O0 SMS messages will be sent unless you opt in again on https://aimez.ai/o0-sms-opt-in.html.

Help / customer care

Reply HELP for help. Customer care contact: hello@aimez.ai.

Mobile information — no third-party sharing or sale

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. aimez does not sell, rent, buy, or transfer mobile phone numbers or SMS opt-in data to third parties, affiliates, or lead generators. Mobile numbers are used only to deliver aimez O0 SMS operational alerts and to process replies through Twilio and US carriers as required for message delivery.
