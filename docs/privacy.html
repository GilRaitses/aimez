aimez — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-22

This Privacy Policy describes how aimez handles information collected through https://aimez.ai/ and the aimez O0 SMS operational alert program.

Website

The public website may collect standard server and analytics logs needed to operate and improve the site. Contact emails you send to aimez are used only to respond to your request.

aimez O0 SMS

aimez O0 SMS sends short hard-stop notices to opted-in mobile numbers when an automated research workflow needs a human decision. Opt-in is only through the public form at https://aimez.ai/o0-sms-opt-in.html (phone number plus an unchecked-by-default SMS consent checkbox).

Mobile numbers — no sharing / no sale

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. We do not sell, rent, or transfer mobile phone numbers or SMS opt-in data to third parties, affiliates, or lead generators. Mobile numbers are used only to deliver aimez O0 SMS operational alerts and to process replies through message delivery providers and US carriers as required for message delivery.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies with operational need. Recipients typically receive a few messages per day or fewer when hard-stops occur.

Message and data rates

Message and data rates may apply. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Data we store for SMS

We store the enrolled phone number and message metadata needed to deliver and verify replies. We do not send marketing SMS.

Opt out / help

Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for help. Support: hello@aimez.ai.
