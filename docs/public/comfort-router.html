Heat routing uses Landsat surface temperature on the street graph (greenR framework, Mahajan Ecological Indicators 2024). Surface temperature is not pedestrian-height air temperature; on parallel avenues the difference a walker feels is governed by upwind conditions the satellite does not see. Built with pax.
