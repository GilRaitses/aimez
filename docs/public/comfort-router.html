Heat routing uses Landsat surface temperature on the street graph (greenR framework, Mahajan Ecological Indicators 2024). Surface temperature is not pedestrian-height air temperature; on parallel avenues the difference a walker feels is governed by upwind conditions the satellite does not see. Thermal layers honesty: MRT is modeled (uses a static LST composite, not per-bin surface temperature); UTCI is provisional (placeholder air temp, proxy wind, placeholder relative humidity); Central Park canopy height is a 12 m DBH proxy, not survey.
