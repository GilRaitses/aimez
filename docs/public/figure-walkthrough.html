§0 · Problem

Urban walking is a graph with hidden load

We fix a Midtown pedestrian graph and ask whether measurable local pressure reorganizes route cost and corridor-level flow—without claiming a general theory of the city.

  • Open-data supply (capacity layers)
  • Field / lived calibration on reference blocks
