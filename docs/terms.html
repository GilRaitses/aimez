aimez O0 SMS program

Program name: aimez O0 SMS (also called O0 SMS).
Operated by: aimez.
Description: a private operational alert program that sends recurring automated SMS hard-stop notices when an aimez research workflow needs a human decision. Messages may include a lane or wave name, a short summary, a recommended next step, and optional links to aimez handoff artifacts. Recipients may reply with keywords such as proceed, shrug, or yes.

This program is not marketing, not affiliate marketing, and not third-party lead generation. Messages are sent only to people who opt in directly with aimez.

SMS consent

Consent for aimez O0 SMS is given directly to aimez on the campaign opt-in page https://aimez.ai/o0-sms-opt-in.html by entering a mobile number and checking an unchecked-by-default SMS consent box. Consent is campaign-specific and is not bundled into a general website registration, purchase, or unrelated agreement.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies with operational need. Recipients typically receive a few messages per day or fewer when hard-stops occur.

Opt out

Reply STOP to any aimez O0 SMS message to end messages. After STOP, no further aimez O0 SMS messages will be sent unless you opt in again at https://aimez.ai/o0-sms-opt-in.html.
